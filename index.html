How The Leaderboard Is Calculated

This page pulls fixtures and results from ESPN's public FIFA World Cup scoreboard, caches the response in this browser for one day, then matches teams to sweepstakes owners and applies the scoring rules from the Excel sheet. Matches that have not been played stay scheduled/upcoming instead of being guessed.
